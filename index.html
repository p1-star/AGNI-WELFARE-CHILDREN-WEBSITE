About Us

Agni Rural Welfare Society is a non-profit organization committed to social service and community development. Our primary focus is to support underprivileged children, rural families, and vulnerable communities through education, care, relief work, and social awareness initiatives.

We believe that social service is a noble responsibility and that collective effort can bring meaningful change to society.

Our Social Activities

As part of our ongoing social initiatives, Agni Rural Welfare Society supports poor school students in the Sundarbans by providing new clothes, study materials, portion food, and essential items.

These activities aim to bring joy, dignity, and a sense of inclusion to children from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.
